Ask ChatGPT

Jan 15, 2025

To say “Ask ChatGPT” should not be insulting as saying “Google it”, it’s just surprising the number of tasks it can perform decently well, beyond what a normal person would expect.

Now that the lesser-known OpenAI gem is trivially available on their app, you can just talk (yes, to the microphone) about the specific work you need to do, sharing tons and tons of context, and the o1 model can consume it all and end up with quite reasonable advice, in 2 minutes at most.
